# Zero-friction capture > careful taxonomy

Design principle for eat: the whole value prop is zero friction. A raw note costs almost nothing (a file, a frontmatter block). A lost insight costs everything — you can never reconstruct a specific 'aha' moment from memory. Therefore: eat asks zero questions, requires zero taxonomy decisions, accepts any content. Taxonomy happens later, during digest, when the cognitive cost of classification is amortized against the already-proven value of the content.
