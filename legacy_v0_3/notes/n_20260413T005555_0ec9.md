# Permanent workflow: research world-class practices BEFORE any work

User behavioral directive (permanent): Before doing ANY significant work, first research world-class best practices and expertise in that specific domain. Then absorb. Then execute.

Pattern: Research → Absorb (myco_inlet) → Execute

This applies to EVERYTHING — not just READMEs:
- Before writing code: research how the best projects structure that kind of code
- Before designing architecture: study how top systems solve the same problem
- Before writing docs: study how the best projects document
- Before optimizing: study what metrics top teams track
- Before testing: study testing strategies of top projects

"有了世界顶尖的专精的技巧和经验之后，再去干相关的工作" — this is a non-negotiable workflow principle. Do not skip research. Do not rely on training weights. Always search, absorb, then build.
