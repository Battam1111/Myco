Contract audit craft 2026-04-15: identified D1-D7 design issues. v0.3.4 scope: D1 (immune skeleton mode detects .myco_state/autoseeded.txt, downgrades L0/L1/L5 CRITICAL->HIGH on fresh substrate), D2 (--exit-on={never,critical,high,concerning} flag on hunger/immune/reflect to decouple mechanical failure from metabolic signal), D3 (integrate git check-ignore into L1/L12/L14 severity), D6 (pyproject.toml dynamic=['version'] from __init__.py + new L29 version consistency lint), D7 (L14 gitignore-aware shared with D3). Deferred to v0.3.5: D4 (lint stratification into 4 categories: mechanical/shipped/metabolic/semantic), D5 (separate exit policies per category). Root insight: current lint conflates mechanical validation, shipped-artifact contract, metabolic monitoring, and semantic review — they have different failure semantics and should not share one exit code. CI kept failing because smoke-install treated cold-substrate metabolic signals as build failures; fix in v0.3.4 makes this architectural, not papered-over with '|| true'.
