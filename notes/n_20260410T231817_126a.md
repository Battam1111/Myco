# Meta-lesson: never skip attack phase

Promoted to debate protocol rule: even when a claim looks self-consistent and elegant, DO NOT skip the attack phase. The Round 1 seven-organ claim looked internally sound — the attacks revealed it was over-fit to biological metaphor at the cost of actual usability. Without attacks, we would have shipped a beautifully-named 7-tool API that agents couldn't use reliably. Attacks are not adversarial; they are the fastest way to find the shape of the truth.
