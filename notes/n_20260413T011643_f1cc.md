Master Storytelling Playbook — 15+ frameworks synthesized.

TOP FRAMEWORK FOR MYCO: RASKIN STRATEGIC NARRATIVE
1. Name undeniable shift: "AI agents generate insights at superhuman speed, but insights vanish when conversation ends"
2. Winners vs losers: Teams who compound AI knowledge vs those who let it evaporate
3. Enemy mindset: "Just re-ask the AI" — belief conversations are disposable
4. Promised Land: knowledge substrate grows smarter every interaction
5. Magic Gifts (product features): only NOW show Myco
6. Evidence: proof it works

9 OPENING LINE PATTERNS:
- Contrarian Truth: "Everyone says AI has perfect memory. It doesn't."
- Undeniable Shift: "AI agents now generate more knowledge per hour than humans. None of it survives."
- Visceral Question: "How many times has your AI re-discovered the same bug?"
- Cost of Inaction: "Every session, your AI starts from zero. That's 100% knowledge loss."
- Three-Word Hook: "AI forgets. Fix it."

8-SECTION README NARRATIVE ARC:
1. Hook (name the shift)
2. Shift (show the change)
3. Pain (agitate the problem)
4. Promised Land (paint the future)
5. Demo (show, don't tell)
6. How (the mechanism)
7. Proof (evidence)
8. CTA (call to action)

UNIFYING PRINCIPLE: Product is never the hero. USER is the hero. Product is the GUIDE. Myco doesn't save the day — it gives the developer the power to save the day.
