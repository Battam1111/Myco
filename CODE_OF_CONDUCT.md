# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Myco is a tool for people who take careful thinking seriously. We hold our community to the same standard: rigorous, honest, and respectful.

We pledge to make participation in the Myco community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, experience level, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be precise and honest in technical claims
- Welcome questions from people at all experience levels
- Give credit where it's due (especially for battle reports and adapter contributions)
- Assume good faith in ambiguous situations
- Accept and offer constructive critique without taking it personally

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Dismissing contributions without explanation
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the project

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.

To report an issue, contact the maintainers via GitHub Discussions (private message) or email.
